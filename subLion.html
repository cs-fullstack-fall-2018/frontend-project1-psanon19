Lion Facts

  1. Lions are the second largest big cat species in the world (behind tigers).
  2. The heaviest lion on record weighed an amazing 375 kg (826 lb).
  3. The roar of a lion can be heard from 8 kilometers (5.0 miles) away.
  4. Most lions found in the wild live in southern and eastern parts of Africa.
